## FAQ (quick-answer block)

**Can foreigners buy property in Belgrade?**
Most can — Serbia uses a reciprocity rule, and we confirm your status up front. If your
country doesn't qualify, a Serbian company can buy on your behalf, completely legally.
→ [Full explanation](02-the-reciprocity-rule-explained.md)

**Do I need to be in Belgrade to buy?**
No. You can buy by power of attorney; many of our clients close from abroad.

**What does it cost beyond the price?**
Typically a 2.5% transfer tax on resale (VAT on new-builds), ~0.4% annual property tax, and
our commission at standard market rate. We lay out every cost before you commit.

**Will buying give me residency?**
Not automatically, but it supports a residency application — and we can help with that.

**I'm American — what about taxes?**
There's no U.S.–Serbia tax treaty, so we introduce you to a cross-border tax advisor to keep
both sides clean.
